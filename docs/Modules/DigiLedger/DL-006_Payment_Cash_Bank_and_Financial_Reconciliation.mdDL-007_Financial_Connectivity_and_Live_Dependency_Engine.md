# Financial Connectivity Rule

Every financial card, summary and amount displayed inside the Lottery Workspace must remain connected to its original business transaction.

No value inside ORBIS should exist without a traceable source.

The following cards must always remain interconnected:

- Cash Balance
- Bank Balance
- Outstanding
- Collections
- Payments
- Daily Sales
- Dispatch
- Returns
- Commission
- TDS
- Ledger
- Statement
- Reports

Selecting any card immediately opens its detailed source.

# Real-Time Synchronization

Whenever a transaction changes,

all connected financial cards automatically update.

Examples:

Dispatch Updated

↓

↓

Statement Updated

↓

Ledger Updated

↓

Dashboard Updated

↓

Reports Updated

↓

Executive Analytics Updated

No manual refresh is required.

---

# Single Source of Truth

Every financial amount originates from one business transaction.

Summary values are calculated from the ledger.

The ledger is calculated from business transactions.

Business transactions are never duplicated.
